Mahesh Babu

The Superstar of Telugu Cinema

Here's a timeline of Mahesh Babu's life and career:

  • 1975 - Born on August 9 in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India.
  • 1979 - Debuts as a child artist in the film "Needa."
  • 1999 - Stars in his first lead role in the film "Raja Kumarudu."
  • 2003 - Gains widespread recognition with the film "Okkadu."
  • 2006 - Achieves massive success with "Pokiri," setting box office records.
  • 2011 - Stars in "Dookudu," one of the highest-grossing Telugu films of the year.
  • 2015 - Stars in "Srimanthudu," which becomes one of his most acclaimed films.
  • 2019 - Wins the National Film Award for "Maharshi."

"Mahesh Babu is one of the most talented and influential actors in Indian cinema, known for his charisma and dedication to his craft." -- Film Critic, Rajeev Masand
